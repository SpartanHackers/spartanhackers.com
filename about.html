About Us

Here's a little about ourselves.

What is Spartan Hackers?

Spartan Hackers exists to foster a community of technological innovation, collaboration, and creation at Michigan State University.

When was Spartan Hackers formed?

Last year, a group of dedicated students got together to host a series of events to introduce each other to valuable hacking skills as well as host MSU's first hackaton: Spartahack.

Who can become a hacker?

Anyone with a computer or a desire to learn can become a hacker. We have weekly meetings and all are invited.
